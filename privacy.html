Privacy Policy (Pocket Drummer)

1. Who we are

Pocket Drummer (“we”, “us”) sells digital drum practice packs online.

2. What data we collect

We may receive and process:

  • Contact information (such as email address) to deliver your purchase and provide support
  • Order information (purchase details, product name, amount, date)
  • Technical data (basic analytics such as page views, device/browser information), if enabled on the website

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data.

3. Payments

Payments are processed by our sales platform/payment provider. We do not store your full payment card details. The payment provider may process payment data to complete your purchase.

4. Why we use your data

We use your data to:

  • deliver your digital purchase
  • provide customer support
  • prevent fraud/abuse
  • keep records for accounting/tax purposes
  • improve our website and products (if analytics are enabled)

5. Legal basis (GDPR)

We process personal data on one or more of the following bases:

  • performance of a contract (to deliver your purchase)
  • legal obligation (accounting/tax)
  • legitimate interests (fraud prevention, basic site improvement)
  • consent (only where applicable, e.g. optional marketing emails)

6. Sharing your data

We only share data with service providers necessary to run the business, such as:

  • the sales/payment platform (for checkout, taxes, downloads)
  • email/support tools (if you contact us)
  • hosting/analytics providers (if used)

We do not sell your personal data.

7. Data retention

We keep purchase and accounting records as long as required by law. Support emails are kept as needed to resolve issues.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location (including EU/EEA), you may have rights to:

  • access your data
  • correct your data
  • delete your data
  • object to processing
  • request data portability

To exercise rights, contact us at: pocket.drummer.guides@gmail.com

9. Cookies

If we use cookies/analytics, we may use them to measure traffic and improve the site. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

10. Contact

Privacy questions: pocket.drummer.guides@gmail.com
